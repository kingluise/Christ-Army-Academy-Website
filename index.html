ABOUT US

Welcome to The Christ Army Academy (CAA)

Christ Army Academy (CAA) is a co-educational institution located in Ikorodu, Lagos State, Nigeria. The academy offers a comprehensive educational experience, encompassing Pre-School, Primary, and Secondary levels. Dedicated to fostering both academic excellence and moral integrity, CAA emphasizes the development of well-rounded individuals equipped for the challenges of the modern world.

  • The school provides quality education with character development.
  • It prepares students for higher education and citizenship.
  • A structured curriculum supports intellectual and spiritual growth.
  • Students are guided to become community leaders
