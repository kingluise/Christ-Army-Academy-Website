Our Admissions Process

A simple guide to enrolling your child at CAA.

1

Application & Inquiry

Begin by filling out our online inquiry form or visiting the school to pick up an application pack.

2

Entrance Examination

Prospective students will undergo an age-appropriate entrance examination to assess their academic readiness.

3

Interview & Assessment

Successful candidates and their parents will be invited for an interview and further assessment.

4

Admission Offer

Upon successful completion of all stages, an admission offer will be extended, along with necessary enrollment documents.

5

Enrollment & Orientation

Complete the enrollment process by submitting required documents and attending our orientation program.

Requirements & Fees

Key information for prospective parents.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous academic records/report cards
  • Passport photographs (child and parents)
  • Medical records

Tuition & Fees

Our tuition structure is competitive and offers excellent value for the quality of education provided. Fees vary by program level.

For detailed fee information, please contact our admissions office or download the full fee schedule.
