Our Academic Curriculum

A comprehensive approach from early years to secondary education.

Creche (Ages 0-2)

Our Creche provides a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for infants and toddlers. Focus is on sensory development, early social interaction, and motor skills through play-based learning.

  • Sensory Play
  • Early Communication
  • Motor Skill Development
  • Personalized Care

Nursery (Ages 2-5)

The Nursery program introduces foundational literacy and numeracy skills, fostering curiosity and independence. Activities are designed to encourage creative expression and social-emotional growth.

  • Phonics & Early Reading
  • Number Recognition
  • Creative Arts & Crafts
  • Social Play & Etiquette

Primary (Ages 5-11)

Our Primary curriculum is robust, covering core subjects with an emphasis on critical thinking and problem-solving. Students engage in project-based learning and extracurricular activities.

  • Mathematics & English
  • Science & Social Studies
  • ICT & French
  • Moral & Vocational Studies

Secondary (Ages 11-16+)

The Secondary section prepares students for national and international examinations. We offer a broad range of subjects, career guidance, and leadership opportunities to shape future leaders.

  • Core Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Humanities (History, Geography, Literature)
  • Commercial Subjects (Economics, Accounting)
  • Advanced ICT & Vocational Skills

Our Learning Philosophy

Fostering holistic growth and academic excellence.

At The Christ Army Academy, we believe in a holistic educational approach that nurtures intellectual, spiritual, social, and emotional development. Our philosophy is centered on:

  • **Student-Centered Learning:** Tailoring education to individual needs and learning styles.
  • **Inquiry-Based Education:** Encouraging curiosity and critical thinking through exploration.
  • **Character Integration:** Weaving moral values and ethical principles into every aspect of learning.
  • **Collaborative Environment:** Promoting teamwork and mutual respect among students and staff.
  • **Global Perspective:** Preparing students to be responsible and compassionate global citizens.
