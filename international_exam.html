IELTS Preparatory Classes

Comprehensive training for the International English Language Testing System, focusing on listening, reading, writing, and speaking skills required for academic and general training modules.

  • Targeted Skill Development
  • Extensive Practice Tests
  • Experienced Tutors
  • Individual Feedback Sessions

TOEFL Preparatory Classes

Preparation for the Test of English as a Foreign Language, designed to assess non-native English speakers' English proficiency for university-level study in English-speaking countries.

  • Integrated Skill Practice
  • Computer-Based Test Simulation
  • Vocabulary & Grammar Enhancement
  • Strategic Test-Taking Tips

SAT Preparatory Classes

In-depth coaching for the Scholastic Assessment Test, covering evidence-based reading and writing, and mathematics sections, to boost scores for college admissions.

  • Critical Reading & Analysis
  • Writing & Language Mastery
  • Math Problem Solving
  • Full-Length Mock Exams

IGCSE Preparatory Classes

Tailored support for the International General Certificate of Secondary Education, providing subject-specific expertise and exam techniques for a wide range of Cambridge IGCSE subjects.

  • Subject-Specific Content Review
  • Past Paper Analysis
  • Exam Technique Workshops
  • Personalized Study Plans
